Let's Get Tracking

Once we've scheduled and are ready to begin, all you have to do is relax, play, and most importantly, have fun!

Drums

I can individually track up to 9 microphones simultaneously, providing the ultimate flexibility. Whether your song calls for roomy, vintage style drums, to huge, punchy style drums, I can make it happen. 

Guitar & Bass

I track guitar & bass by A/B splitting the signal - one signal goes to your amp which is mic'd (stereo optional), and the other goes to a premium DI box for digital amplifier simulation. I also use an attenuatable (to reduce volume) cabinet, so you can hit the sweet spot of your tube amp without damaging our hearing!

Vocals

The pinnacle of a good mix is a good vocal performance. My job is to make sure that the singer is relaxed and focused so they can deliver the power and emotion to make the song come alive.

EDITING & MIXING 

After tracking, I start by reviewing the tracking session and noting any editing needs that need to occur. Once complete I will begin mixing in-the-box with award winning plugins. 

MIX FEEDBACK & NEW REVISIONS

After the initial mix is complete, I will send to you for review and feedback. This process can occur up to three times before we go into Mastering

FINAL MASTERING & PUBLISHING

Once approved, I apply final mastering processing to the final track. I will export the final master in WAV format with individual STEMS optional. All you have to do is publish.

Got Questions?

Why don't you charge by the hour?

I find that having an hourly rate creates a conflict of interest. With this model, my interest is to make more money by working longer, and your interest is to save money by compromising.

Instead, I charge by the project by providing an up-front quote once I understand the scope of the project and your needs. This way, we can spend as much time as necessary getting it right, without the pressure of being on the clock*.

*This is with the exception of editing. Comprehensive editing is billed by the hour. Don't worry, if you check out my 3 Steps to Nailing Your Performances on Tracking Day Guide, this won't be a problem. 

How long will my project take?

It depends on the size of the project of course. I provide my services during after-work hours. For individual song projects, I try to get a first revision within two weeks. Full albums can take over a month. EP's are somewhere in the middle.

What if I'm not happy with my mix?

I give my golden promise to do everything I can to exceed your expectations. But sometimes, things happen, and projects don't work out. If this is the case, we can stop the project with no questions asked, and I will bill the lesser amount of either a (reduced) $30/hour rate of time invested, or the initially quoted project price.
